Once upon a pond…

Deep in the woods lies Beaverville — a cozy little colony where the water is cool, the birch is crunchy, and every beaver has a dam to call home. Life was perfect. Until the humans showed up.

Then came the humans 😤

With clipboards, bulldozers, and absolutely zero respect for quality dam architecture, the humans want to drain the pond. The beavers have other plans. Big, wooden, water-holding plans.

Chomp. Build. Defend. 🪵

Gnaw down trees, haul logs, and engineer the mightiest dam the forest has ever seen. Outsmart the humans, flood their plans, and protect the colony. Every log counts in the battle for Beaverville.
